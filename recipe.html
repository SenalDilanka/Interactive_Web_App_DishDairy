Classic Creamy Pasta

Simple ingredients, rich flavor, and easy preparation.

Browse All Recipes
Dinner 30 minutes Serves 2-3 people

Ingredients

  • 250g pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 cup cream
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté chopped garlic.
  3. Add cream and stir well over low heat.
  4. Mix in parmesan cheese until smooth.
  5. Add pasta to the sauce and coat evenly.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and parsley before serving.
